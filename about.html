About SHAIDS - Student Hive for Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

Welcome to SHAIDS, the Student Hive for Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, an exciting initiative driven by passionate students. Established in 2023, SHAIDS is dedicated to fostering a vibrant community that explores the fascinating realms of AI and Data Science while embracing the spirit of collaboration.


Our Vision

At SHAIDS, our goal is to enrich the college experience for students by conducting a diverse range of events encompassing technical, cultural, and sports activities. We aim to provide opportunities for students to enhance their skills, showcase their talents, and engage in a holistic learning journey.


What we Offer?

  • Technical Excellence: Through a dynamic lineup of workshops, webinars, hackathons, and coding competitions, we empower students with practical skills and insights in AI and Data Science. Our experienced mentors and industry experts guide our members to stay at the forefront of technological advancements.

  • Cultural Diversity: Beyond technology, SHAIDS celebrates the diverse talents of our student body. Our cultural events, talent shows, and artistic exhibitions create a platform for showcasing creativity and artistic brilliance.

  • Sports and Fitness: We believe in the holistic development of our members. Our sports team organizes sports events, tournaments, and fitness activities to promote a healthy lifestyle and foster a sense of sportsmanship.

  • Networking and Community: SHAIDS serves as a hub for like-minded individuals passionate about AI and Data Science. By joining us, you become a part of a close-knit community where collaboration, knowledge sharing, and networking thrive.

Get Involved

SHAIDS is an inclusive committee that welcomes students from all disciplines, whether you are an AI enthusiast, data wizard, artist, or sports aficionado. Join our vibrant community to explore the exciting world of AI and Data Science, discover your talents, and make lasting memories.
